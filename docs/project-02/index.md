# Project 02

A 15-minute city platform that helps residents identify essential services within walking or cycling distance from any location in their city.

## Overview

The 15-minute city concept promotes urban sustainability by ensuring all essential services are accessible within a 15-minute walk or bike ride. This platform makes this concept actionable through comprehensive service mapping and accessibility analysis.

## Key Features

- **Service Mapping**: Interactive map showing all essential services (grocery, healthcare, education, etc.)
- **Accessibility Scoring**: Calculate accessibility scores for any address
- **Route Planning**: Find optimal walking and cycling routes to services
- **Community Input**: Residents can add or verify service locations

## Technology Stack

- Frontend: Vue.js, Leaflet
- Backend: Python, FastAPI
- Data Processing: Python GeoPandas
- Routing: OSRM

## Impact

Pilot testing in three neighborhoods revealed service gaps that led to the planning of new community centers and improved public transit connections.
